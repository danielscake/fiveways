The Five Ways to Well Being

Earn Points Using These Methods!

Connect

Use Watcard to attend events like bingo night, feminist poetry slam, info sessions, club fair, joining a club, networking sessions!

Be Active

Scan Watcard when going to the gym, shoe tag classes, sports teams, squash, or swimming.

Take Notice

Keep a journal on our website or in the physical journal, you can do this either monthly, weekly, or daily and you earn points for length, and how often.

Keep Learning

Sign out a book at the library, or join a club that teaches you to do something, set goals for learning certain things and make to-do lists.

Give

Sign up for volunteer experiences or donate money through website/watcard.
